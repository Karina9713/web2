WHO ARE WE?

Founded by Canadian environmentalist Lisa Christensen in 2000, Hong Kong Cleanup began with a one-day beach cleanup event. Under Lisa's leadership, participation has increased by an average of 65% annually, making it the foremost cleanup organization in the region.

We have engaged over 1,000,000 participants and removed over 11,000,000 kilograms of trash.

Hong Kong Cleanup operates as a community, offering a year-long education platform that collaborates with companies, schools, government entities, and individuals to provide practical solutions for sustainable living every day of the year.

What we offer?

Throughout the year, we host managed cleanup events for corporations seeking meaningful team-building experiences with their employees. We are the first and favorite organisation in Hong Kong providing this service.

We are the official coordinator of the global cleanup movement for Ocean Conservancy’s International Coastal Cleanup and Let’s Do It! World Cleanup, sanctioned by UNEP.

Committed to making a positive impact. Recently, we have also become the official #TeamSeas partner for the region, leading free weekly events for individuals and families, aiming for transformative change in our oceans and the communities and wildlife that rely on them.

OUR MISSION

More than two decades of data and volunteer feedback have highlighted the urgent need for increased community involvement and action. Trash poses a serious threat to the health of our oceans, coastlines, country parks, economy, and people. It pervades every corner and will persist unless we alter our habits. Our missions are-

  • Empowering Hong Kong citizens to actively safeguard our coastal areas, country parks, and city streets by keeping them clean and free of trash.
  • Raising awareness and fostering understanding that communities play a crucial role in shaping attitudes towards environmental protection.
  • Inspiring positive change in waste management, littering, pollution, and promoting enhanced public-private cooperation to reduce waste production in Hong Kong.

Our ultimate vision is a future where cleanups are no longer necessary. We aspire to raise awareness and drive change to significantly reduce trash generation and prevent any waste from polluting our natural ecosystems.
