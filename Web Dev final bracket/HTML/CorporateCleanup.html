BOOK US FOR A CORPORATE CLEANUP

The Hong Kong Cleanup expert events team is available for companies who wish to organise a meaningful, fun, team building day out for staff, clients, or families. By engaging our services you are not only supporting your company’s commitment to the environment, staff engagement, staff retention, and social responsibility, you are also directly supporting the work of Hong Kong Cleanup.

Email us a proposal to book us for a managed cleanup for your company: info@HKcleanup.org

What we offer:

  • Registration – Complete all paperwork for enrolment, including team registration and government approvals as required
  • Advisory – Advise client on best location, transport, food and beverage, safety, and scheduling options
  • Planning – Event rundown creation, options for transport, food and beverage, and liaising with client on registrations and teams, weather arrangements, tips on what to wear, bring and preparation
  • Internal communications support – provide ideas and verbiage for inviting participants
  • Teambuilding activities – plan and lead warm up, education and team trash collecting competition
  • Event management – Hong Kong Cleanup staff to be onsite, providing expert briefing and education, cleanup materials distribution, photography, data support, awards presentations
  • Data Management – Support data collection during event and collate data post-event for inclusion in reports using our CleanSwell App
  • Post event – Send photographs and event recap to client, publish acknowledgment on social media

Cleanup materials provided:

  • Cleanup Materials: Gloves, bags for all participants, litter pickers, ect.
  • Award Certificate: Team will be presented with a certificate of recognition
  • Event materials: Event schedule, first aid, signage, education materials
  • Photographs: Hong Kong Cleanup will take photographs of the day’s event for your use and sharing
  • Post-event report: With data, overview, link to google drive photo’s and social media recap

Recommended app to store your cleanup data:

CLEANSWELL APP enables you to upload your data to Ocean Conservancy’s global ocean trash database. These data deliver a local and global snapshot of ocean trash, providing researchers and policy-makers insight to inform solution

Our ultimate vision is a future where cleanups are no longer necessary. We aspire to raise awareness and drive change to significantly reduce trash generation and prevent any waste from polluting our natural ecosystems.
